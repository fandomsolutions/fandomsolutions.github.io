THE PROCESS

We don't just sell you an app. Our team at Fandom Solutions will work with
your students and alumni to develop the solution perfect for your campus.

Collaboration

The most important step in building the perfect solution is getting your fans involved in its construction. We set up platforms to communicate with your fan base to ensure they get the right features for your solution.

Prototyping

Your colors, your mascots, your logos; your look. We'll make sure your students and alumni love the way your solution is designed and experienced through prototyping based on the feedback of your fan base.

Testing

We'll make sure your fans are happy with their solution by holding a closed beta test prior to your launch. Students and alumni will be able to test their new app on iOS and Android devices to make sure all of the desired functionality is included.

Production

Once your app launches, we'll be right there next to you to monitor satisfaction, feedback, and requests for new features. We'll also do everything we can to continue to improve your solution with upgrades and support.
